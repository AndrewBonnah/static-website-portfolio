AI vs Human Text: Quick Analysis

Last time I generated 20,000 GPT-2 text sequences and compared them to human-written corpora. This time, we're keeping it simple and looking at two things: lexical diversity variability (how varied the words are) and sentence-length variability (how sentences flow).

Image from earlier cursory analysis, placing our dataset amongst other known AI vs Human datasets.

The idea is simple: human text tends to sit in a narrower range, AI text spreads out more, and this shows why global metrics alone aren't enough to tell AI from human. Think of this as a quick map before diving deeper.
